Our Purpose

The purpose of the Citizen Voice is to
provide a secure platform where individuals
can anonymously report incidents related to social
issues such as police brutality, political oppression
vandalism, theft, and domestic violence, fostering
a safer and more just community for all.

Your Rights

As a citizen of Nigeria, you are entitled
to a range of fundamental rights enshrined in
the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
These rights are designed to ensure your dignity,
freedom, and security. Understanding and exercising your
rights is crucial in fostering a just and equitable society.

Your Secret

At Citizen Voice, we understand the importance of
privacy and security, especially when reporting
sensitive situations. We are committed to protecting your
identity and ensuring that your personal information remains
safe and confidential.
